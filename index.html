About Regina

I'm Regina Contini — a Registered Dietitian and trained chef with a passion for the connection between food, culture, and wellbeing. My approach to nutrition is rooted in the belief that eating well should feel joyful, not restrictive.

Whether you're managing a health condition, working toward a fitness goal, or simply want to eat better and feel better, I'm here to meet you where you are.

✿
Why a dandelion?

The dandelion is for my Nonna from Sardinia — who once walked into our overgrown yard with a bucket, gathered what the rest of us called weeds, and turned them into a beautiful meal of olive oil, garlic, and dandelion leaves. That's when I realized that real nourishment is rarely complicated — sometimes it's been in your own backyard all along.
